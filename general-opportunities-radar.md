# Open Opportunities Radar (General - All Fields, All Sectors)

Scan the open web for every type of opportunity regardless of field, sector, or geography. Cast the widest possible net.

## Categories to Scan

### Abstract & Paper Calls
- Academic conferences across all disciplines (health, tech, engineering, social sciences, policy, climate, education)
- Journal special issues (open for submissions)
- Poster sessions, late-breaker abstracts, panel proposals
- White paper calls (industry, think tanks, NGOs)

### Grants & Funding
- Government grants (all levels: local, national, multi-lateral)
- Foundation grants (large and small)
- Research grants (basic, applied, translational)
- Innovation/startup grants (non-dilutive)
- Corporate grant programs
- UN, World Bank, AfDB, ADB, EU Horizon, USAID, DFID/FCDO, SIDA, GIZ, etc.
- Venture philanthropy / impact investing
- Micro-grants and rapid-response funding

### Awards & Prizes
- Innovation awards (any sector)
- Social impact awards
- Design awards
- Engineering/technology prizes
- Essay/photo/video competitions
- Fellowship awards (individuals or organizations)
- Lifetime achievement / early career awards
- Cash prizes, recognition-only, or in-kind

### Fellowships & Residencies
- Research fellowships (postdoc, senior, visiting)
- Policy fellowships
- Journalism/media fellowships
- Artist/creative residencies
- Entrepreneur-in-residence programs
- Leadership development programs
- Embassy/cultural exchange fellowships

### Jobs & Consulting
- Full-time, part-time, contract, freelance
- Remote, hybrid, on-site
- Short-term consultancies (tenders, RFPs, RFQs)
- Board/advisory positions
- Secondment opportunities

### Accelerators & Incubators
- Startup accelerators (equity or non-equity)
- Venture studios
- Social enterprise incubators
- Lab-to-market programs
- University spinout support
- Corporate innovation labs

### Training & Capacity Building
- Free/paid online courses and certifications
- Workshops, bootcamps, summer schools
- Professional development programs
- Exchange programs (international)
- Technical assistance programs

### Partnerships & Collaborations
- Consortium calls (Horizon Europe, etc.)
- Multi-stakeholder partnership initiatives
- Data-sharing collaborations
- Open-source project contributions
- Sister-city/municipal partnerships
- University-industry collaborations

### Events & Networking
- Hackathons, datathons, codeathons
- Unconferences, bar camps
- Pitch competitions
- Trade fairs and exhibitions
- Delegation visits and matchmaking events

## For Each Finding, Report:

1. **Name** + URL
2. **Type** (abstract, grant, award, fellowship, job, accelerator, event, etc.)
3. **Deadline** (confirmed date or "rolling" or estimated range)
4. **Field/Sector** (what discipline or industry)
5. **Geography** (global, specific region, country, or city)
6. **Level** (student, early-career, mid-career, senior, organization, any)
7. **Alignment** (which specific project or interest it fits, or note if broad/unrestricted)
8. **What's needed** (abstract, proposal, application form, nomination, CV, portfolio, etc.)
9. **Value** (cash amount, funding size, prize value, stipend, exposure, in-kind, certificate)
10. **Urgency** (high/medium/low based on deadline proximity to today)

## Scanning Tactics

- Check conference aggregators and academic calendars
- Search funding databases (GrantStation, fundsforNGOs, SPIN, Pivot, ResearchProfessional)
- Monitor foundation websites and newsletters
- Browse LinkedIn and professional association job boards
- Follow UN, World Bank, AU, EU, AfDB procurement portals
- Scan professional society newsletters in relevant fields
- Check government innovation agency websites
- Look at past recipients' lists for pattern-matching to find next cycles
- Search for "call for abstracts," "call for proposals," "request for proposals," "open call," "request for applications," "nominate," "apply now" + upcoming deadline month
- Check social media (X/Twitter, LinkedIn) of major funders and organizations
