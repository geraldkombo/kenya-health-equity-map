# Kenya Health-Tech & Equity: Open Opportunities Radar

Scan Kenyan and Africa-wide channels for open calls, abstract submissions, funding, and partnership opportunities relevant to health equity mapping, digital public goods, and community health monitoring.

## Channels to Monitor

### Kenyan Government & Parastatal
- **Ministry of Health Kenya** – moh.go.ke (tenders, grants, data partnerships, KHIS/DHIS2 integrations)
- **Council of Governors (CoG)** – cog.go.ke (county-level health financing, CHMT partnerships)
- **Kenya Medical Research Institute (KEMRI)** – kemri.go.ke (research collaborations, data sharing)
- **National Commission for Science, Technology & Innovation (NACOSTI)** – nacosti.go.ke (research permits, innovation grants)
- **Kenya Health Information System (KHIS/DHIS2)** – hiskenya.org (data integration opportunities)
- **Kenya Open Data Initiative** – opendata.go.ke (open data publishing partnerships)

### Pan-African & Continental
- **Africa CDC** – africacdc.org (grants, data sharing, health informatics)
- **African Union** – au.int (Digital Transformation Strategy, health data initiatives)
- **Smart Africa** – smartafrica.org (digital health, DPG alignment)
- **Africa Health-Tech Accelerator** – africahealthaccelerator.com
- **African Health Observatory (AHO)** – aho.afro.who.int

- **Consortium of Universities for Global Health (CUGH)** – cugh.org (abstracts usually Q3-Q4)
- **Health Systems Global (HSG) Symposium** – healthsystemsglobal.org (biennial, Kenya edition when hosted in Africa)

## For each opportunity found, report:
1. **Name** of opportunity + URL
2. **Deadline** (confirmed or estimated)
3. **Alignment** with this project (health equity mapping, DPG, maternal health, CHMT planning, etc.)
4. **What's needed** (abstract, full proposal, demo, partnership)
5. **Urgency** (high/medium/low based on deadline proximity)

## Always cross-check:
- Does the opportunity mention Kenya, East Africa, or Sub-Saharan Africa as a focus geography?
- Does it explicitly fund digital health, open data, GIS/mapping, or community-based monitoring?
- Is the submission format compatible with existing collateral (briefs, method page, demo video)?
- Would the project's open-source / DPG status be an advantage or disadvantage?
